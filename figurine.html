My work for the model:

This figurine was modelled as part of a class project. The guidlines statet that we were only allowed to use wire to construct the "skeleton", which was mounted on a wooden base. In lieu of plaster or gypsum, this wire frame was wrapped in painter's tape, and finally covered in white paint.
