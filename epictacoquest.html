About the game:

Epic Taco Quest is a little game that came into being as a class assignment. The project called for teams of two to create games inspired by arcade classics from the era of the Atari 2600.
Micky, the underground construction worker, is ready for a well-earned break, but... he accidentally left his lunch taco in the break-room!

It's now up to you to navigate Micky to the object of his desire. Dig your way back into the earth, around obstacles and dangerous creatures, ever closer to your delicious meal. But most importantly - do all this before the surrounding earth becomes too instable!
