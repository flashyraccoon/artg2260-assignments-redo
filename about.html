About Me

Hi! My name is Anna.

I'm a student at Northeastern University majoring in Game Design. I transferred to Northeastern in fall 2017, after completing my first year at the Zurich University of the Arts in my home country, Switzerland.
My path to become a game designer had to endure somewhat of a detour; prior to my studies in Game Design, I was working towards a degree in Ancient Studies in my hometown at the University of Basel.
But now I'm finally well on my way, and it's been an adventure! Basel to Zurich to Boston, so much has changed in just two short years - and I plan to keep going strong.

Right now I'm still finding my way and building my skillset, but my dream is to work a stable job at a great studio, and with a great team! Currently, I've set my eyes on level or environment design, 3D asset creation, or game design; but who knows where I'll eventually end up.
