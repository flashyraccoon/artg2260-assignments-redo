About the game:

NIRAL is a drag-and-drop puzzle game developed as a first-semester class-project in fall 2016.

As the newcomer to the weirdly unique, but conflict-ridden tribes of the now overgrown "Toni Areal", the hero of the game is tasked with bringing peace to these strange people by uniting them in the face of a common threat.

Solve puzzles and the tribespeople's problems by stuffing them into your bag, and getting them where they need to go!​​

My work for the game:

My contributions to the project included team-based tasks such as designing the game mechanics, quest design. I further took on individual work on level design, asset creation, and graphic design for the closing credits, as well as occasional language support for translations from German to English.

Further group-tasks included concept design, decision making with regards to the story, art style, and team coordination in groups of varying sizes.
