My work for the clip:

Working with 3ds Max, I modelled the floating island with tha lighthouse and surrounded it with clouds. Incorporating two 3D models from fellow students, I arranged all of the models together in an animation. Additionally, I enriched the clip with an audiotrack, including free-to-use sound effects and the "whistle theme" from the game Deadly Premonition.

Tools I used:

  • 3ds Max
  • After Effects
